Made for modern browsers

The Extention is not perfect, there may be bugs but I will keep updating. Wiki Redesign is made for Modern browsers so I suggest using latest version of browsers.

Your Privacy

Privacy is a critical concern for everyone and i assure you the 'wiki redesigned' do not pry on any of your data. The extention itself works offline and do not have any internet related services.

  • There are some minor bugs related to Image Viewer
